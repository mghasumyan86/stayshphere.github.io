About StaySphere B&B

History of our Bed and Breakfast

In 2015, amid the vibrant cityscape of Vancouver, Mariam Ghasumyan founded StaySphere Bed and Breakfast, a sanctuary that transcends the conventional lodging experience. It emerged as a collection of rooms, not just offering accommodation, but a commitment to providing warmth and a sense of home in every detail.

The early chapters tell a story woven with the enticing aromas of homemade breakfasts, shared narratives, and the joy of crafting enduring memories. The allure of StaySphere quickly permeates, prompting expansion to incorporate new rooms and amenities, all while preserving the soulful ambiance that defines the essence of StaySphere.

Today, StaySphere stands as a symbol of harmonious coexistence between contemporary comforts and timeless allure. Our dedication to innovation resonates through cutting-edge reservation technologies, dynamic room displays, and an intuitively designed website – all meticulously crafted to enhance your overall experience.

Yet, StaySphere transcends its role as a haven for travelers; it pulsates as the heartbeat of the local community. Within these walls, events flourish, collaborations with neighboring businesses thrive, and initiatives for a vibrant local life find a steadfast partner in StaySphere.

Stepping into StaySphere isn't merely an act of becoming a guest; it's an invitation to be part of a narrative – a narrative characterized by warmth, growth, and the continuous evolution of StaySphere Bed and Breakfast.
